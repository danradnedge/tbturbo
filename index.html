TB Turbo Chest Counter

This extension enhances your gaming experience in Total Battle by automating chest collection and providing detailed player contribution tracking.

Key Features:

  • Automated Chest Opening with click replication.
  • OCR Technology to read and record chest data.
  • Creates and updates a CSV spreadsheet with player contributions.
  • Real-time dashboard for player statistics.
  • Customizable clan wealth and chest targets.
  • Full database management (rename, delete, merge players).
  • Multi-Language OCR support.
  • User-friendly popup interface.
  • Data Import/Export functionality.

How It Works:

  1. Capture the chest opening location.
  2. Set number of chests to open.
  3. Extension clicks and uses OCR to capture data.
  4. View/manage data in-game or export to Google Sheets.
